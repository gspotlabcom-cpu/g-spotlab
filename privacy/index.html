We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers, IP address, and other data required to provide ad services.

Purchases are handled by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store any payment information.

If you contact us by email, we may receive your email address and any information you voluntarily submit.
