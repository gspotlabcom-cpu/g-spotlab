1. Introduction

G-Spot Lab (“we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, and protect your data when you use our mobile game Merge Universe and any related services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Google Play Games Services (Optional)

If you choose to sign in with Google Play Games, we may receive:

  • Player ID
  • Google Play Games profile name
  • Profile avatar
  • Cloud Save data
  • Leaderboard and achievements data

2.2. Local Game Data

We store gameplay progress, settings, inventory, boosters, in-game currency and other game-related information locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to us or third parties.

2.3. Device and Technical Data

Google AdMob and Google Play Services may automatically collect:

  • device model
  • operating system version
  • system language
  • time zone
  • IP address (region detection only)
  • Google Advertising ID
  • crash logs and diagnostic data

2.4. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers, IP address and other data required to provide ad services and measure their performance.

2.5. In-App Purchases

Purchases are handled by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store any payment information such as card numbers or billing addresses.

2.6. Customer Support

If you contact us by email, we may receive your email address and any information you voluntarily submit as part of your request.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information described above to:

  • operate and improve the game and its features;
  • enable optional Google Play Games functionality;
  • display ads through Google AdMob and measure their performance;
  • diagnose crashes and technical issues;
  • improve stability, security and overall user experience;
  • respond to user support requests.

4. Sharing Information

We only share data with the following service providers, to the extent necessary to provide our Services:

  • Google Play Services (including Google Play Games)
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Billing

We do not sell your personal data or share it with any other third parties.

5. Data Retention

Local game data remains on your device until you uninstall the game or clear its data. Google Play Games and Google Play Billing retain data in accordance with Google’s own policies and settings in your Google account.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our game is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe that we may have collected data from a child, please contact us so that we can delete it.

7. Your Rights

  • You may delete local game data by uninstalling the app or clearing its data.
  • You may disconnect Google Play Games at any time in your Google account.
  • You may manage or delete Google Play Games data using Google’s privacy tools.
  • You can limit or reset ad personalization in your device settings.
